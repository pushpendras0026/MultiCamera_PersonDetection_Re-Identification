\begin{frame}{Conclusion}
    \small
    \begin{block}{Main Contributions}
        \begin{itemize}\setlength\itemsep{4pt}
            \item Addressed the critical issue of moving distractors in video-based Person Re-Identification, a major limitation in prior works.
            \item Proposed the \textbf{Spatial Memory Module (SMM)} to dynamically detect and filter out background motion patterns at the frame level.
            \item Proposed the \textbf{Temporal Memory Module (TMM)} to effectively aggregate the refined spatial features across video sequences.
            \item Achieved state-of-the-art or highly competitive performance on major benchmarks like MARS and iLIDS-VID.
        \end{itemize}
    \end{block}
\end{frame}
